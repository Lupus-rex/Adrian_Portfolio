About Me

Hello, my name is Adrian Mckenzie.

I've been in the field of sales and customer service since 2015. I'm upbeat, energetic and I do my best to stay positive. I was introduced to coding through a friend who was intrigued by showing them how to work around a computer, and explaining it well enough where they can understand it. My friend asked if I ever thought of becoming a programmer, and at first I never thought about until I did my research. After doing some extensive research I began to see myself in that field, envision the pros and the cons, and see how much work it would take to become great in this profession. Suddenly, I decided to commit to learning how to code and what the steps to take to become a great web developer. When I told my friend that I was going to pursue a career in web development, she was excited and recommended this bootcamp that would teach me everything. I love to challenge and push myself to learn new skills and become great at them, so when an opportunity presents itself to me I seize it and give it 100% of my effort.
